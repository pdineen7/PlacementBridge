Exact MVP

Version one does six jobs exceptionally well.

PlacementBridge is intentionally focused. It is not trying to replace an SIS, LMS, or full IEP platform in version one. It solves the operational pain of district-to-provider coordination.

👥

Student workspaces

Each student has one secure workspace with the relevant district team and approved outside collaborators.

🔐

Role-based access

District admins, case managers, tutors, providers, and placements only see what they are supposed to see.

💬

Secure messaging

Threaded communication is tied to the student record, searchable, auditable, and much cleaner than email.

📁

Document vault

Upload, preview, request, and version progress reports, service logs, releases, evaluations, and notes.

✅

Tasks and deadlines

Create accountability for missing reports, expiring consents, follow-ups, and student-specific next steps.

🧾

Audit trail

Every sensitive action is captured so district leaders can see who uploaded, accessed, changed, or responded.

Pricing hypothesis

District SaaS, not individual teacher subscriptions.

PlacementBridge is designed to be sold to district leadership with pilot-first pricing and a clean expansion path.

Pilot
$6k–$9k

Annual pricing for a limited pilot with a small number of active student workspaces and outside partners.

  • Up to 25 active student workspaces
  • 2 external partner organizations
  • District onboarding included
  • Prove value before expanding
Core district
$18k–$30k

Best fit for special education departments that coordinate frequent reporting, tutoring, or placement handoffs.

  • Up to 150 active workspaces
  • Unlimited district users
  • Audit logs and permissions
  • Tasks, messaging, document workflows
Collaborative / multi-school
$35k+

For larger implementations needing shared governance, stronger reporting, and structured rollout support.

  • Higher volume workflows
  • Custom implementation support
  • Multiple programs or schools
  • Expanded admin controls
